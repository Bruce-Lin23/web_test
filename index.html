Financial Aid

Stanford is committed to making education affordable for all students through a robust financial aid program.

  • 72% of students receive financial aid toward the cost of attendance
  • Tuition covered for undergrads with family incomes below $150,000
  • Tuition, room, & board covered for undergrads with family incomes below $100,000
  • Graduate aid available based on available funds and academic merit

About

Who We Are

Stanford University is a world-renowned research institution dedicated to advancing knowledge, cultivating leadership, and driving innovation to address the world’s most pressing challenges. Founded in 1885 by Leland and Jane Stanford in memory of their only child, the university was built on the belief that education is a force for the public good. Today, Stanford is home to a vibrant community of more than 16,000 students, exceptional faculty, and visionary scholars who collaborate across disciplines to spark discoveries and transform ideas into action. With a commitment to academic excellence, diversity, and inclusion, Stanford fosters an environment where students are empowered to think critically, explore boldly, and make meaningful contributions to society. From groundbreaking research and entrepreneurial ventures to public service and global partnerships, we continue to shape the future through curiosity, compassion, and a shared purpose to improve lives everywhere.
